Purpose Statement

The purpose of this project is to help people lose weight, maintain health in their body which will help to save money on medication in the future, to have good attitude towards life, be healthy and happy, have good families, will be able to better provide for a family. If there is health a father can provide a mother can raise children, children can be happy and grow into good people. This web site will also provide: Good health practice skills. Easy way to lose a “bear belly” for men, men’s health - main focus on testosterone and it’s influence towards a man’s body in general, why do we need it, how to maintain. Also, my personal preference is a set of exercises for fun, fitness and skill – a couple of elements from Parkour.
