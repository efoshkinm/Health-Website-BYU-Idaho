Visitor Analysis and Persona

yandex.ru

URL: http://yandex.ru

Target Audience

The main target audience is going to be middle class, middle age people that have a higher education, good income, office employees, married people, everybody who needs information. Particularly this website is oriented on Russia audience. I used this website a lot.

So, Yandex is a web portal that provides users with meaningfull information related to every aspect of life.
For example on page 1 you may notice the Search bar, access to Maps, Videos, eStore(Market), Images, Weather.
Page 2 provides us with good News feed.It is very important for the target audience. News are structured with best practices Typography, Alignment, Proximity and Contrast.
Page 3 provides Curreancy rates of USD, EUR, RUR. This section is very important, because currience is changed daily and not only business people need it but also an average person, as well as families.
Page 4 provides meaningfull links to resources of real estate, car purchase, Job opportunities and usefull Apps for Android.
Overall the web page provides users with meaningfull information that is necessary for daily tasks. It is good for families as well as for business. Mobile version of the web page provides structured presentation of information. It keeps the interest and is very comfortable to use. Fonts, texture and proximity facilitate understanding of the content. Colors of the page make it enjoyable.

Persona

My audience persona will be represanted in an average man that has a higher education: bachelors or Masters degree. Income is above average. The name of the man will be Max. On the picture the person is clean and well dressed, that means that he is succesfull in life and needs only products that will help him to become even more successfull. The products should be very efficient. He works as a Web Developer. Very communicative. Knows Different languages. Has active life. Keeps himself in shape by going to the GYM. Uses all kinds of technologies possible, mainly Apple products: Tablets, iPhones. Computer is a part of his life. Is dating a girl he likes. Has a car.
