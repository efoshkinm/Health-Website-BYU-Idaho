Purpose Statement

The purpose of this web site is to provide accurate temperature for various audiance. The majority of which will be regular citizens, pilots, military, media. Also the site will provide a conversion chart Celsius to Fahrenhiet and back chart for the people travaling the world. Also web site will provide information for every hour of the day. wind, longitude, latitude, etc.

This is going to be achived through a constant information update. Providing a conversion chart through a Python script. All the information is gather and stored in one file called weather.json.
